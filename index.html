Services

We can help you with a variety of immigration needs. Whether you like to visit Canada or immigrate to Canada or even become a proud Canadian, we are here to help. We can also help in immigration appeals with Immigration Appeal Division.

Visit

Apply for a visitor visa to visit Canada as a tourist. Explore Canada's beautiful landscapes and meet your friends and family.

Study

Get advice regarding what to study and where. Admission advising, and case specific counselling for study permit.

Immigrate

Get permanent residence status in Canada. We assist in all PR programs run by Government of Canada.

Work

Have a job offer and would like to apply for a work permit? We can assist in obtaining different work permits.

Citizenship

Apply for Canadian citizenship and become a proud Canadian. Enjoy the perks and responsibilities of being a Canadian.

Renewals

Renew your immigration documents in time - study permits, work permits, visitor visas, PR cards, etc. Always have a valid status.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Some of the common questions that we get asked often!

  • Are you authorized by Immigration Refugees and Citizenship Canada to represent clients?

    Yes, we are licensed by and are in good standing with Immigration Consultants of Canada Regulatory Council (ICCRC) and are authorized to represent clients to Immigration Refugees Citizenship Canada (IRCC), Canada Border Services Agency (CBSA), Immigration Appeal Division (IAD), and Immigration and Refugee Board (IRB). You can verify our standing at https://iccrc-crcic.ca/find-a-professional/

  • Do you provide online consultation and services?

    Yes, we love technology and make full use of it to serve our clients remotely so they stay safe and healthy.

  • Since you are based in Canada, do you assist clients in Canada only or all over the world?

    Our clients come from different parts of the world. We assist clients no matter whether they are in Canada or outside.

  • How do I avail your services?

    You can do a free assessment if you would like to know your eligibility. Upon review, we will let you know if you are eligible for the immigration program you are interested in. Then we can setup a consultation to discuss further. If you have specific questions, then you can directly book a consultation. If you would like to retain us for our services, then you can directly contact us via email and we would be elated to assist you.

  • I am an international student interested in studying in Canada? Can you help?

    Absolutely! We can help in advising you regarding universities/colleges and help in admission and visa process. Our team is made of university graduates with industry experience so they can provide good advice.

  • I want to move to Canada permanently? How can I do that?

    Government of Canada has numerous PR pathways such as express entry, caregiver pilots, etc. Contact us to know which one will be the best for you.
